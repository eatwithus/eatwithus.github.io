online ordering

OFT delivery

Deliverwithus revolutionized on the fly delivery in India to support 'Pickup and Drop' delivery concept. Our professionally trained executives provide customers the flexibility and comfort to get your order delivered while you are on the go.

cash on delivery

State of the art mobile based technology enables deliverwithus in depositing COD money in your bank account within 24 hours of collection. Daily reconciliation reports and other metrics help our clients in keeping an exact track of their capital.

instant cash refund

Timely delivery is one of the biggest challenge facing the e-commerce industry and our commitment to customer service led to the launch of P&D (Pick & Drop). Under P&D, the quality check is done at customer's doorstep and cash is returned instantly.

real time tracking

Time between placing an online order and getting it delivered seems too long for most customers. They want to know every movement of the order. The real-time information helps in reducing customer care calls and ensures happy customers.
